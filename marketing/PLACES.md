# Direct marketing places — by persona

Tactical list of where to put My Very Own in front of real users in each persona's
audience. Honor the global rules: **no Reddit** (per Roger's call), no engagement bait,
no fabricated user quotes, persona-specific banned words from the content factory still apply.

**Excluded from direct outreach** (partner-led only per safety lock):
- **Hearth** (emotional support) — only via licensed bereavement counselors / mental health partners
- **Rainbow Bridge** (pet loss) — only via vet hospice orgs / pet bereavement partners
- **Little One** (pregnancy) — needs doula/midwife endorsement before any outreach

For these three, the marketing path is **partnership**, not posting. Don't pitch them to general audiences.

## TOP 5 MOVES TO SHIP THIS WEEK

1. **Pinterest** — instant intent traffic for The Promise (wedding) + Green Thumb (gardening) + Study Buddy. Take the 20 OG cards we already generated, post as pins, link `/start/<persona>`. Free, ~5K reach per pin in niche categories.

2. **X auto-poster + manual replies** — auto-poster is live for broadcasts. Roger spends 30 min/day in lifting Twitter, fitness Twitter, K-pop stan Twitter, and FinTwit replying to relevant convos with persona angles. Highest conversion-per-effort.

3. **TikTok creator DMs** — find 30 micro-creators (5K-50K followers) per niche in #fitnesstok, #studytok, #kpoptiktok. DM offering 1 year free + revshare or affiliate. Even 3-5% response rate = 5-10 collabs.

4. **Hacker News "Show HN"** — once-and-done but powerful. Post Iron Brother angle: "I built an AI fitness coach that doesn't write your program." Tuesday morning ET, hunter audience overlaps with Iron + Player + Ledger personas.

5. **Product Hunt launch** — schedule 2-3 weeks out. Build hunter relationships first. AI productivity products have been winning launch days through 2025-2026.
