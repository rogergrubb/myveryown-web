## NOTES FOR ROGER

- **Tweet 1 names a real category fight** (every fitness app wants to write your program) which signals you've actually used these. Gym Twitter respects builders who lift; they'll smell it if you haven't.
- **Tweet 3 is THE most important tweet.** Lifting culture HATES overpromising AI. Listing what it can't do builds more trust than any feature list.
- **Tweet 5: "roast the system prompt"** is a builder-flex that lifting Twitter respects. They don't want polish — they want someone who'll iterate from feedback.
- **DO NOT use**: "transform your body", "see results", "dad bod to gains", "we built the only AI that...". Banned by the V2 prompt and banned by lifting culture.
- **DO NOT post a meme as the image.** The OG card looks editorial; lifting Twitter wants editorial. Memes feel try-hard from a brand account.

## REPLY PLAYBOOK

- "Does it know percentages?" → Yes. Same with RPE, RIR, set/rep schemes. It doesn't *prescribe* them, but if you tell it "today was a 9 RPE single", it remembers.
- "What about diet?" → Different persona. Fuel Daily handles macros. Iron Brother stays in the lifting lane.
- "Can it replace my coach?" → No, and we don't want it to. If you have a good coach, this is for between sessions.
- "It's just ChatGPT with a system prompt." → Shared memory across 20 personas, persistent across sessions. Try the persona switch on /chat — it's not a system prompt trick.
