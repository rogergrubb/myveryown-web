# Product Hunt launch draft

## Best launch day
Tuesday or Wednesday, 12:01 AM PT. Product Hunt voting closes at 11:59 PM PT.
Tuesday gives you full weekday eyeballs; Wednesday is the second-best slot.

Avoid: Monday (Pacific Time spillover from previous Sunday's posts), Friday (lower
traffic), and weekends.

## Required assets

### Tagline (max 60 chars)
"20 AI companions. Each one remembers you privately."

### Description (max 260 chars)
"My Very Own is 20 cinematic AI companions—K-pop bestie, fitness coach, wedding planner, gardening buddy, and 16 more. Each one keeps its own private memory of you. One subscription unlocks them all. Free 7-day trial."

### Long description (full launch page body)

**The pitch:**

> Most AI tools make you re-explain your whole life every time you open them. We built 20 specialists who each remember their slice of you.
>
> Iron Brother knows your PRs and tweaked back. Scarlet knows your late-night thoughts. The Promise knows what your mom said about the venue. They don't share that with each other unless you toggle them to.

**Who it's for:**

- People who already use AI for specific things (fitness, writing, gaming, finance) but hate the context loss
- People who want emotional/companion AI but want it to feel grounded in their actual life
- People who think one giant LLM answering everything badly isn't the right product shape

**Three things to try first:**

1. Start with the persona that fits the most of your life. Tell it about you. Come back tomorrow — it remembers.
2. Tap a different persona mid-session. The new one doesn't know what you said to the previous one (private threads). That's by design.
3. Generate an image. Each persona gives you a different aesthetic. (3 free per day.)

**What we deliberately don't do:**

- We don't offer Hearth, Rainbow Bridge, or Little One via direct outreach. Those personas exist but only ship through licensed mental-health, vet hospice, and doula partnerships. Some products shouldn't grow via ads.
- No fabricated user reviews. No fake testimonials.
- No "AI assistant" framing. These are companions, each with a specific voice and domain.

### Maker comment (post within 30 minutes of launch)

> Hey hunters! Roger here, solo builder behind My Very Own. Couple things worth knowing:
>
> 1. The 20 personas were chosen based on overlapping needs — domains where memory across sessions actually matters (fitness, writing, wedding planning, faith, etc.) vs. ad-hoc Q&A where it doesn't. If a domain is "I'll explain it once and we're done," it's not in here.
>
> 2. Private threads are the default. The opt-in shared-mind toggle is for users who actively want their personas to compare notes. We learned the hard way that NOT having privacy as the default felt invasive.
>
> 3. We're a 7-day free trial, $9.99/mo or $71.88/year after. One subscription unlocks all 20.
>
> 4. Image generation is included — 3/day on free, 100/month on paid.
>
> Happy to answer questions about architecture, persona design choices, or the very specific decision NOT to market certain personas.

### Categories to submit under

- Artificial Intelligence
- Productivity
- Lifestyle (secondary)

### Pre-launch hunter outreach

- DM 5-10 known PH hunters in the AI space 2 weeks before launch with a sneak preview
- Ask 1-2 specific friends in the AI / dev community to be hunters/featured supporters
- Don't ask for upvotes; ask for honest feedback

### Day-of action plan (PT timezone)

- 12:01 AM — Post goes live
- 12:30 AM — Post maker comment
- 6:00 AM — Email your 100 closest contacts asking for honest feedback
- 9:00 AM — Update X / Threads / LinkedIn
- All day — Respond to every comment within 30 min
- 6:00 PM — Final push if you're in the top 5

### What NOT to do

- Don't beg for upvotes
- Don't farm friends for fake comments
- Don't promise features you haven't built ("coming soon" is allowed; "available now" must be true)
- Don't compare to specific competitors by name in the launch copy
