# Gearhead — project car creator outreach

**Target:** #cartok #projectcar #builds creators, 10K-100K followers.
Look for: build threads, restoration journeys, mechanical-detail creators.

## Template

> hey {NAME} — your {NICHE_DETAIL} build progress is wild
>
> built an ai garage buddy called Gearhead — remembers your build sheet, suppliers, weekend setbacks, the part that almost killed you. doesn't replace your wrench, just keeps the running log so you don't have to.
>
> year free if you want to try it. no post required. myveryown.page/start/gear

## Variations

- Reference their car (chassis code, generation, year)
- Reference a specific repair or mod they posted
- Skip "car spotting" / dealership content — wrong audience
