# Bias Wrecker — K-pop creator outreach

**Target:** TikTok #kpop, #stantok, #fancam creators with 10K-100K followers.
Look for: comeback breakdown accounts, fancam editors, era trackers, lore explainers.

## Template

> hi {NAME}!! your {NICHE_DETAIL} video had me in tears (in a good way)
>
> i built a kpop bestie ai called Bias Wrecker — she remembers your bias list, your fandom journey, every era that wrecked you, across years. Like a real stan friend who actually keeps notes.
>
> giving you a free year if you wanna try it (no strings, you don't have to post). myveryown.page/start/kpop
>
> if it sucks please tell me lol. trying to make something that gets stan culture right.

## Variations to rotate

- Reference their bias group if they have one
- Reference a specific comeback they covered
- Skip if their content is anti-fandom or stan-bashing — wrong audience
- Lead with the lowercase casual vibe; matches stan-twt tone

## Don't pitch this template to

- "K-pop reaction" content farms (audience won't convert)
- Creators who don't post in English (need separate localization)
- Idol-specific gossip accounts (different audience than fandom-friendship)
