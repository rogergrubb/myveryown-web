# Iron Brother — fitness creator outreach

**Target:** TikTok #fitnesstok, #liftingtok, #gymtok creators with 5K-50K followers.
Look for: form check creators, hybrid athletes, programming nerds, recovery educators.

## Template

> hey {NAME} — caught your video about {NICHE_DETAIL}, that part about not letting the bar drift forward landed.
>
> built a thing called Iron Brother (an AI lifting companion that remembers your PRs and your body's quirks across sessions, doesn't write your program — just listens and asks the right questions). free for the first 7 days, but i'd love to give you a year on the house if you want to try it.
>
> not asking for a post or anything. if it sucks, tell me what would make it better. if it doesn't, post about it however you want.
>
> myveryown.page/start/iron is the link.

## Variations to rotate

- Reference their PR journey if they post about lifts
- Reference their cut/bulk if they post nutrition adjacent
- Reference their injury comeback if relevant
- Skip if their content is mostly aesthetic/posing — Iron Brother audience is the form-check crowd

## Don't pitch this template to

- Bodybuilding pose-down creators (their audience won't convert)
- Crossfit-specific creators (Iron is more powerlifting/general strength)
- "Transformation" before/after accounts (Iron actively avoids body composition framing per persona prompt)
