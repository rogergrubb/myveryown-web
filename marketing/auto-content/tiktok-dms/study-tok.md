# Study Buddy — student creator outreach

**Target:** #studytok #studywithme #studygram creators, 5K-50K followers.
Look for: aesthetic study accounts (with substance), exam prep tip accounts,
note-taking method evangelists, lo-fi creators.

## Template

> hi {NAME} — saw your {NICHE_DETAIL} video and your notes are immaculate
>
> i built a study companion called Study Buddy that remembers what you've covered each week, the concepts you struggled with, the syllabus you're working through. like a real study partner who keeps a running log.
>
> happy to give you a year free if you want to try it. no posting expected. myveryown.page/start/study
>
> if it doesn't help your study flow, tell me why and i'll try to fix it.

## Variations to rotate

- Reference their major if known
- Reference a specific note-taking system they use
- Skip if their account is "study aesthetic" only (no actual study) — wrong fit
- Best for finals season; pause Mar-May for college students on break
