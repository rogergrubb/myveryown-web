# Ink & Quill — writing creator outreach

**Target:** #writingcommunity #amwriting creators, 5K-50K. NaNoWriMo, manuscript craft,
character development creators.

## Template

> hey {NAME} — your {NICHE_DETAIL} thread/post hit me. been chewing on it.
>
> built a writing companion called Ink & Quill — remembers your characters, your plot threads, the voice you're building, across drafts. like a writing partner who never forgets your manuscript.
>
> a year free if you want to try it for your current WIP. no expectations. myveryown.page/start/ink

## Variations

- Reference their genre (literary, romance, sff, etc.)
- Reference their current WIP if mentioned in bio
- Skip "publishing industry hot takes" creators (different audience)
