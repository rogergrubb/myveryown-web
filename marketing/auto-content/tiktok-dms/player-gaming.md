# Player Two — gaming creator outreach

**Target:** Gaming TikTok creators 10K-100K followers covering specific games (League, Valorant, Apex, Genshin, Elden Ring etc.). Game-specific is better than general.

## Template

> yo {NAME} — that {NICHE_DETAIL} clip was clean
>
> built an ai gaming buddy called Player Two — remembers your builds, your team comps, your chokepoints across games and seasons. doesn't tell you how to play, just keeps notes like a co-op friend would.
>
> would love to give you a year free if you want to mess around with it. zero strings, no post needed. myveryown.page/start/player
>
> tear it up if it sucks, that's how i'll fix it.

## Variations to rotate

- Reference their main game / role
- Reference a specific clip detail (the play, the build, the matchup)
- Skip if their content is mostly gaming-news commentary — wrong audience
- Best slot: PT evening DMs (when gamers are online)
