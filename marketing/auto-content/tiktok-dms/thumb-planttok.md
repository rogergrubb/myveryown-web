# Green Thumb — gardening creator outreach

**Target:** #planttok #garentok creators 10K-100K. Vegetable gardeners specifically
(higher conversion than houseplant aesthetic accounts).

## Template

> hey {NAME} — your {NICHE_DETAIL} from the garden tour was the kind of bed I want to build next year
>
> built a garden companion called Green Thumb — remembers your zone, your beds, what you transplanted in March, what's about to bolt. private to your garden conversations only.
>
> year free if you want to try it. no strings. myveryown.page/start/thumb

## Variations

- Reference their zone if visible
- Reference a specific crop or technique
- Skip pure-aesthetic plant accounts (audience won't convert)
