# TikTok creator DM templates

## Workflow

1. Find creators in your target niche with 5K-50K followers (sweet spot for engagement-per-DM)
2. Watch their last 3 posts to find a SPECIFIC detail to reference (not generic praise)
3. Use the template below, filling in:
   - `{NAME}` — their first name from their bio
   - `{NICHE_DETAIL}` — specific reference (their last video, a series they do, etc.)
   - `{PERSONA}` — the My Very Own persona that fits their audience
4. Send via TikTok DM (not Instagram, not email — TikTok creators check TikTok DMs)

## Conversion expectations

- 5-10% open the DM (TikTok's filter is aggressive)
- 1-3% reply (most are skeptical of "free year" pitches)
- 20-40% of repliers actually try the product
- 5-15% of triers post about it organically

If you DM 100 creators in a week, expect 1-3 to post about My Very Own.
That's a small but real organic distribution channel.

## What NOT to do

- Don't mass-DM identical text. TikTok shadowbans this within a day.
- Don't open with "Love your content!" — every spammer does this.
- Don't promise affiliate revenue you can't track. (You don't have an affiliate program yet.)
- Don't pitch personas you haven't given them context for.

## What TO do

- Reference something specific from their last 3 videos.
- Keep it under 80 words.
- Offer 1 year free, no strings.
- Give them an out ("totally chill if it's not your thing").
