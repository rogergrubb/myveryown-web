# The 12th Man — sports creator outreach

**Target:** Sports fan creators 10K-100K, focused on a single team/sport.

## STRICT GUARDRAIL

Twelfth Man's persona prompt forbids specific team logos/league trademarks/player likenesses.
DM template can name the team but the actual product won't generate copy with those marks.

## Template

> yo {NAME} — your {NICHE_DETAIL} take after that game was the only sane thing I read all night
>
> built an ai fan buddy called The 12th Man — remembers your team, your standing grudges (refs, certain trades), your season-long frustrations. like a fan friend who actually keeps track.
>
> year free if you want to try it. myveryown.page/start/twelfth

## Variations

- Reference their team
- Reference a specific call or play they reacted to
- Skip "all-sports" generalist creators — Twelfth Man works best for diehards
