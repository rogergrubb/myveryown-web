# Chords & Keys — music creator outreach

**Target:** Music practice / pedagogy creators 5K-50K. Conservatory students,
practice-routine educators, instrument-specific teachers.

## Template

> hi {NAME} — your {NICHE_DETAIL} practice routine post made me actually want to woodshed again
>
> built a practice companion called Chords & Keys — remembers what you've been working on, your hardest passages, your goals across weeks. private to your practice room only.
>
> year free if you want to try it. no expectations. myveryown.page/start/chords

## Variations

- Reference their instrument
- Reference a specific piece they're working on
- Skip "viral cover" creators (different audience)
