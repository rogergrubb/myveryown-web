# Iron Brother — fitness newsletter sponsorship pitches

## Newsletter targets

| Newsletter | Sub count | Notes | Audience |
|---|---|---|---|
| BarBend | ~150K | Paid sponsorship, premium rates | Powerlifting, hybrid athletes |
| Stronger By Science | ~80K | High-quality readership | Programming nerds, science-minded lifters |
| Garage Gym Reviews | ~60K | Founder-friendly | Home gym owners |
| Boostcamp | ~40K | Powerlifting focus | Programming users |
| Mind Pump newsletter | ~30K | Podcast crossover | General strength training |

## Pitch template

> Subject: Sponsorship interest — Iron Brother, an AI lifting companion (no programs sold)
>
> Hi {NAME},
>
> Founder of My Very Own here. We've built 20 specialist AI companions, and one of them — Iron Brother — is an AI lifting buddy that remembers your PRs, your tweaks, your training history across sessions. It actively does NOT write programs (lifters hate that). It just listens, asks the right questions, remembers what you said about your back last week.
>
> No "transform your body" copy. No "see results." It's the anti-fitness-app.
>
> I'm looking at sponsoring 1-2 sends to your audience to see how it lands. Standard sponsored-mention with a UTM-tagged link to myveryown.page/start/iron. Free 7-day trial, $9.99/mo after.
>
> What are your rates and slots?
>
> Roger
> myveryown.page

## Variations

- Reference a recent newsletter article they wrote about programming or recovery
- Mention specific lifters they've featured — show you actually read it
- For Stronger By Science specifically, lead with the science framing (memory namespace per persona is a real architectural choice, not just marketing)
