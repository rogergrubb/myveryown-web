# Newsletter sponsorship outreach

## How sponsorship works

You email a newsletter operator with a fitted sponsorship pitch. Most niche newsletters
(under 50K subs) charge $150-500 per sponsored mention. Larger ones ($500-2000) want
a longer dedicated section.

Best targets: Sub list 5K-50K, niche-aligned to one of your personas, owner is a real
person (not a corp), they accept sponsored content.

## Find them

- **fitness:** BarBend (paid), Stronger By Science (paid), Mind Pump newsletter
- **personal finance:** Money With Katie, Cool Toolings, The Daily Upside, The Hustle
- **writing:** The Write Practice, Anne R. Allen
- **gardening:** Mother Earth News, Joe Gardener
- **wedding:** Wedding Planning Society, Sapphire Society

## Negotiation

- Don't pay full rack rate on first mention. Offer 60% of asking + a longer relationship
- Ask for a UTM-tagged link so you can attribute conversion
- 1-2 sponsored mentions before you assess

## Conversion expectations

- Niche fit + 5K subs ≈ 30-100 visits per send
- 5-10% of visits convert to trial signup
- 5-10% of trials convert to paid
- Per send: 1-5 paying users, $10-50 LTV per
- Hard to break even on first send; sponsorships are repeat-relationship plays
