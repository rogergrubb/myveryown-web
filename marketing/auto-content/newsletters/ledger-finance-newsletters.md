## STRICT GUARDRAIL FOR LEDGER PITCHES

The Ledger is a "talk through it, never advise" companion. Pitches MUST avoid:
- "Investment" or "investment advisor"
- "Build wealth"
- "Optimize your portfolio"
- "Returns" or "beat the market"

These are banned in the persona prompt itself. Sponsorship copy must respect that.
