3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the Service. Generate AI responses, maintain persistent memory across sessions, authenticate you, show your conversation history.
  • Process payments. Charge subscriptions via Stripe, handle refunds.
  • Improve the Service. Analyze aggregate usage patterns to fix bugs and improve features. We do NOT use your individual conversation content to train AI models.
  • Security and abuse prevention. Detect fraud, rate-limit abuse, block bad actors.
  • Communicate with you. Send service emails (account confirmations, magic-link logins, subscription receipts). We will not send marketing emails without your explicit opt-in consent.
  • Comply with legal obligations. Respond to lawful requests from law enforcement, comply with tax and regulatory requirements.

4. How Your Conversations Are Used by AI Providers

Your conversations are processed by Google's Gemini API to generate AI responses. When we send a prompt to Gemini, we transmit the conversation context (your message, the persona's system prompt, and relevant memory from prior conversations) to Google's servers.

Per Google's Gemini API terms for paid usage, your conversation data is not used to train Google's public AI models. Google may retain prompts and responses for abuse monitoring and to meet their own legal obligations. See Google's Gemini API Terms for details.

We also use Cortex, our own memory layer (hosted on Railway), to store long-term conversation memory so your AI companion remembers you. Cortex is a first-party system — your memories stay within our infrastructure and are keyed to your user ID.

7.1 California Residents (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • Right to know what personal information we've collected about you, the sources, the purposes, and the categories of third parties we share it with
  • Right to delete personal information we've collected
  • Right to correct inaccurate information
  • Right to opt out of sale or sharing. We do not sell or share personal information as those terms are defined in the CCPA. If that ever changes, we will provide a "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" link on our homepage.
  • Right to limit use of sensitive personal information. We do not use sensitive personal information for purposes other than providing the Service.
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights. We will not charge you a different price or provide a different quality of service because you exercised your privacy rights.

You may designate an authorized agent to make requests on your behalf. We will verify your identity before fulfilling any request by asking you to confirm information we already have on file.

7.2 EU, UK, and Swiss Residents (GDPR / UK GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, our legal basis for processing your information is:

  • Performance of contract (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR) — processing necessary to provide the Service you requested
  • Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) — security, abuse prevention, and service improvement, balanced against your rights
  • Legal obligation (Article 6(1)(c) GDPR) — tax records, responding to lawful requests
  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR) — for any marketing emails (we will ask first)

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In the UK, that's the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO). In the EU, it's the supervisory authority in your country of residence.

Data transfers from the EU/UK to the United States are protected by Standard Contractual Clauses with our service providers where applicable.

9. Security

We take reasonable security measures to protect your information:

  • All data in transit is encrypted with TLS 1.2 or higher (HTTPS everywhere)
  • Passwords are not stored — we use magic-link email authentication
  • Session tokens are signed with strong cryptographic secrets and expire
  • Payment data is handled entirely by Stripe and never touches our servers in raw form
  • Database backups are encrypted at rest
  • Access to production systems is limited to authorized personnel

No system is 100% secure. We will notify affected users and applicable authorities promptly in the event of a data breach involving personal information, as required by applicable law.

10. Cookies and Tracking

We use a minimal set of cookies:

We do not use third-party advertising, analytics, or social media tracking cookies. We do not participate in the IAB Transparency and Consent Framework because we don't need it.

Most browsers let you block or delete cookies. Blocking essential cookies may prevent the Service from working.

11. Do Not Track

Some browsers send a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. Because there is no common industry standard for interpreting DNT, we do not currently respond differently based on DNT signals. We treat all users the same and don't use third-party tracking regardless.
